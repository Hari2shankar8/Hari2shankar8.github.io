Harishankar

Developer


I’m a  developer and graphic designer living in Bhopal, INDIA. I spend
my days with my hands in many different areas of development from
programming (C & C++) to  engineering (HTML & CSS), digital accessibility,                                         user experience and visual design.
I’m extremely passionate about development and design in all it’s
forms and helping small businesses and artisans build and improve their
online presence. Aside from development, I enjoy spending my time with
my friends, , and my laptop, taking photos,watching movies & playing video games.
